Since 2002, The Wentcher Foundation has been providing financial support to highly talented, economically disadvantaged students bound for U.S. colleges and universities. Through its creation, founder Ernest Wentcher sought not only to improve the lives of individual scholars but to help foster a generation of thoughtful, civic-minded young leaders equipped with the tools to make their own marks on the world. Today, carrying on Mr. Wentcher’s legacy, The Wentcher Foundation continues to grow, offering over 50 scholarships across three programs each year. As we move ahead, we at the foundation continue to draw inspiration from our remarkable scholars, many of whom are now thriving professionals, academics and civil servants already showing their commitment to paying it forward.
